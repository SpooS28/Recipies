Shavige Uppitt Recipe

Ingredients for boiling:

  • 4 cup water
  • 1 tsp oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cup (100 gram) rice vermicelli / akki shavige

Ingredients for tempering:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 2 tbsp cashew / kaju
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 dried red chilli, broken
  • few curry leaves
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Instructions

LET'S MAKE THE DOUGH:

  1. Firstly, in a saucepan boil 4 cup water, 1 tsp oil and ½ tsp salt.
  2. Once the water comes to a boil add in 2 cup rice vermicelli.
  3. Boil for 2 minutes, making sure it is cooked well.
  4. Drain off the vermicelli and cool completely.
  5. In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and roast 2 tbsp peanuts until it turns crunchy.
  6. Also, roast 2 tbsp cashew until it turns golden brown.
  7. Keep the roasted cashew and peanuts aside.
  8. In the same oil splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp chana dal, 1 tsp urad dal, 1 dried red chilli and few curry leaves.
  9. Now add 1 inch ginger, 2 chilli and saute slightly.
  10. Further, add 1 onion and saute until it softens well.
  11. Also, add ¼ tsp turmeric and ½ tsp salt. saute until it turns aromatic.
  12. Now add in boiled vermicelli and mix gently.
  13. Cover and simmer for 2 minutes or until flavours are absorbed.
  14. Add in 1 tbsp lemon juice and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.
  15. Finally, enjoy shavige uppittu with chutney or as it is.
