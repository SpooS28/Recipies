Carrot Halwa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kilogram carrots or 9 cups tightly packed grated carrots
  • 6 tablespoons Ghee or clarified butter
  • 2 cups khoya or mawa - grated or crumbled (evaporated milk solids) or 400 to 410 grams
  • 1 to 1.25 cups sugar or add as required
  • 1.5 to 2 teaspoons cardamom powder or 8 to 9 green cardamoms – powdered in a mortar-pestle
  • 1 tablespoon pistachios, chopped or slivered – optional
  • ⅓ cup cashews, or add as required
  • ⅓ cup golden raisins or add as required

Instructions

LET'S MAKE THE DOUGH:

  1. Heat 6 tablespoons ghee in a deep bottomed heavy pan or kadai. Add 9 cups tightly packed grated carrots.
  2. grated carrots in pan
  3. On a low heat, sauté carrots in ghee, stirring often until they turn tender and are cooked – about 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. grated carrots being sautéed in ghee. Add 2 cups of crumbled or grated khoya (a.k.a. mawa or evaporated milk solids).
  5. crumbled soft khoya pieces on top of sautéed carrots in pan.First mix thoroughly.
  6. khoya mixed well with carrots. Then add 1 to 1.25 cups sugar. Adjust the amount of sugar depending upon the sweetness of your khoya. If your khoya is sweet, start with just 1 cup of sugar.
  7. mound of sugar on carrot mixture Combine and mix well.
  8. sugar mixed with carrots You will see that both the khoya and sugar will melt.
  9. sugar has melted and carrot mixture looks liquid. Now add ⅓ cup of cashews, ⅓ cup golden raisins, 1 tablespoon pistachios (chopped) and 1.5 to 2 teaspoons cardamom powder.
  10. dry fruits and cardamom powder on carrot halwa mixture in pan. Mix again and, stirring often, simmer gajar halwa on low heat. The halwa will start thickening, reducing and leave the edges of the pan. When you see these signs, switch off the heat.
  11. gajar halwa made with khoya thickened and cooked in pan.Serve gajar ka halwa either warm or chilled.
