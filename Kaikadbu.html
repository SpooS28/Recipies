Kayi Kadubu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1.5 - 2 cup water (depends on rice flour quality)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup powdered jaggery
  • 2 tsp ghee
  • 1 - 2 cardamom
  • 1 tsp salt (or as per your taste)
  • thick plastic sheet or banana leaf

Instructions for making kayi kadubu:

How to Make Kayi Kadubu at Home

  1. Boil the water in a wide pan along with 1/2 tsp ghee and salt.
  2. When it starts boiling add in rice flour all at once. You can add a tsp of wheat flour (or maida) to avoid the kadubu breaking.
  3. Mix well for a minute and switch off the stove. Cover and set it aside.
  4. Now parallely transfer the grated coconut and cardamom seeds in to mixer grinder and dry grind it.
  5. Now transfer the ground coconut into a frying pan. Add in powdered jaggery and heat it.
  6. Stir well for few minutes under medium flame. When it becomes a soft lump or when the excess water is dried it is done. Do not make it too dry because after cooling it will become hard. Switch off the stove. Hoorana or stuffing is ready. Keep it aside.
  7. By now the rice flour dough would be warm. Grease your fingers with ghee and knead it well. The dough should be soft but stiff.
  8. Now grease the plastic sheet or banana leaf with ghee and place a lemon sized ball.
  9. Grease your fingers or a rolling pin and pat it or roll it into a small circle.
  10. Place 1 tbsp of hoorana or stuffing in the middle
  11. Fold it and seal the edges. Like this finish making 5 - 6 kayi kadubu (using half portion of the dough).
  12. Place them on a greased plate or on a banana leaf and steam cook it for 8 - 10 minutes.
  13. When it is cooking, let us proceed with making of modaka or rice modak. Again grease the sheet, pat the dough and this time place the stuffing in the middle.
  14. Carefully take it in the hand and bring the edges together and seal it. Like this finish making all the modaks.
  15. By now our kayi kadubu is steam cooked. Take them out carefully.
  16. Arrange the modaks on a greased plate (I used idli plates) and steam cook it for 8 - 10 minutes. Enjoy your 2 in 1 kayi kadubu and rice modak with ghee.
